Illustration work

Isometric Illustration

Isometric illustration is a type of 3D illustration, but it does not have vanishing point as lines are parallel. This work is an isometric illustration of a process. I create a scene of students on their way to school. The Colours and shape of buildings are based on Algonquin College, and I added my idea in some parts to show a daily life at school.
